Demo BioSimDB


What is it?

This is a demo application showcasing technology for uploading and exploring biomolecular simulation data.

The aim is to develop a service that will provide storage, display and visualisation of biomolecular simulation provenance. We will focus on automated collection of multiple of steps involved in the building and preparing of systems for molecular dynamics simulations.

How it works

This demo database can be used to upload simulation topology and trajectory files. The full provenance of your simulation setup can also be uploaded by creating an AiiDA archive file.

Who is behind it?

BioSimDB is part of the PSDI project, an intiative to connect data infrastructure used in the physical sciences.

Get Involved!

If you are interested in FAIR and open data in the biomolecular simulation community, please get involved and help us develop, test and grow BioSimDB.
