How to produce and submit your own simulation provenance?

A simple outline:

  1. Install our MD provenance tool
  2. Perform your molecular system build and simulation setup
  3. Save your provenance file
  4. View your simulation provenance
  5. Upload and share your simulation and provenance data
  6. Explore your simulation entries
